About YouthRise Tunisia

Empowering next generation of Tunisian leaders

Our Mission

YouthRise Tunisia is dedicated to empowering Tunisian youth with skills they need to succeed in today's competitive job market. We focus on promoting decent work and economic growth in alignment with Sustainable Development Goal 8 (SDG 8).

Our Vision

We envision a Tunisia where every young person has access to quality education, decent work opportunities, and resources needed to build a successful future.
